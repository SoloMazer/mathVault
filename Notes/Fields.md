### def:
A field is a ring in which every non-zero element is a unit.
### Alternate def:
A commutative division ring is called a field.
#### Examples:
1. $\mathbb{C},\mathbb{R},\text{and }\mathbb{Q}$ are fields, while $\mathbb{Z}$ is not a field.
