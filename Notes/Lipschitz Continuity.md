### def:
A function $f:\mathbb{R}\to \mathbb{R}$ is called as Lipschtiz continuous if $$
|f(X_{1})-f(x_{2})|\leq L\:|x_{1}-x_{2}|
$$
and L is the Lipschtiz constant.
